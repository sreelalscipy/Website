Keep Away from Direct Sunlight

Prolonged exposure to sunlight can fade and crack wood. Place your furniture away from windows or use curtains to protect it.

Clean with care

Use a soft, dry cloth or a slightly damp microfiber cloth to remove dust. Avoid harsh chemicals that can damage the finish.

Maintain with polishing

Polish your furniture every few months with quality wood polish to preserve its shine and protect it from moisture.
